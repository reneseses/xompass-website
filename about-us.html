Our Story - Driven by passion, humility and customer delight to make IOT a reality for everyone

About Us

Our Vision is bring industrial connectivity at par with consumer market - Connect 20B Industrial sensor systems by 2030.

Our Mission is Make IOT, analytics implementation a reality for the organizations everyday.

In July 2015, the co-founders from a top research university met two industry executives at an Internet of Things conference in San Francisco. We had the same passion of transforming the industry with easily deployable SaaS solutions for complex industrial systems. Xompass was formed with a simple vision to make IOT reality by solving the problems around cost-effective, scalable and rapid IOT implementation for organizations. Xompass founders quickly understood that there is only limited market for another "platform", but there is a huge opportunity to fill the gap between existing sensors data, IOT, Analytics for field and operational excellence.

Xompass FaaS (Field Intelligence as a service) coined in 2016 was the first attempt in the industry to develop a "product centric" approach for IOT implementation. Xompass took on some ambitious industrial challenges in the industry with the help of partners. Today Xompass products are used in some of the most advanced industrial systems to make IOT a reality through predictive maintenance and incipient fault detection that are saving millions for organizations.

Most organizations looking to demonstrate value from IOT, analytics - now can do it few minutes with Xompass FaaS products. Xompass engineers operate with the culture of driving "customer delight" with our FaaS product centric design thinking. Our partners share the same values to make us win together.

We are industry experts, differentiated thinkers, problem solvers, humble listeners, and hard working individuals that want to make your IOT, Analytics dream a reality today!.
