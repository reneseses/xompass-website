Data Collection

Easily collect enormous volumes of data from sensors or other devices, leveraging our unique cloud platform technology and IoT data integration. The Xompass solution easily collects all your sensor data regardless of the size or amount. It doesn't matter if you produce 1 Mb/day or 1 Tb/day.

Xompass provides a platform where you can integrate all your information from your assets - from infrastructure to business - no matter where your asset or device is located, no matter the type of connection protocol or device.

Real-Time Data

Generating real-time information is becoming more and more important in the decision-making process. The Xompass cloud solution allows sensor data to be obtained from various assets, and processes them in real time.

Through analysis in real time, it is possible to generate alerts, notifications, and business indicators, which maintain a constant update of the information in your plant.

Data Centralization

Each monitored asset contains a large amount of data sensors, which are stored based on model and business rules. Depending on the frequency and amount of sensors, the database is constantly growing. Xompass provides a platform that can centralize all this information, allowing for simple and fast analysis.

Generating dashboard indicators that consider many assets, historical queries spanning long periods of time, and analyze events and alerts for all assets is only possible through an architecture of robust, reliable, and secure data, generating responses in only seconds.

Data Management

Expression rules make it easy to apply logic to a condition reported from an asset..

Xompass provides three major capabilities to process and store data: RTU and meter configuration service, IoT rules engine, and IoT data model.

Alerts

Xompass enables efficient and flexible real-time alert management, allowing you to use pre-built as well as customizable alerts.

You can assign individual notification policies for each alert. Alert delivery: Email, SMS or HTTP/Web Service.
